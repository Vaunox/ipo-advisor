HOW IPO ADVISOR WORKS

See the issue.
Then see the signal.

IPO Advisor follows an issue from the upcoming calendar through listing, bringing the relevant information into a focused decision workflow—not a trading terminal.

A more deliberate way to follow an IPO.

The app organises what is available at each stage of an IPO. A verdict appears only when enough information exists to support one—and it remains your decision to make.

01 / CALENDAR

Follow what is coming

Review upcoming and open mainboard issues alongside important dates, issue structure and valuation context. Before the book closes, the app watches; it does not pretend to know the outcome.

02 / SIGNAL

Read a considered view

When the necessary information is in hand, IPO Advisor evaluates the available evidence and presents an APPLY, MARGINAL, SKIP or insufficient-signal view.

03 / CONTEXT

Understand what moved it

Grounded reasons and contributing factors make the call legible. You can see the evidence supporting the view rather than accept an unexplained score.

04 / REVIEW

Learn from the record

Use the history view to compare previous verdicts with actual outcomes. Reliability is a continuing responsibility, not a claim made once.

A CLEAR BOUNDARY

Information first. Your decision always.

IPO Advisor is advisory only. It does not place orders, connect to a broker or make promises about a listing-day outcome.
