THE QUANT GENESIS STANDARD

Useful tools
should earn your trust.

IPO Advisor gives independent investors a clearer way to understand an IPO. We build around transparency, uncertainty and the boundary between useful context and investment advice.

Three principles behind every screen.

Quant Genesis is building its first product with a straightforward standard: a financial research tool should make its limits as clear as its capabilities.

01 / EVIDENCE

Evidence over excitement

IPO decisions deserve more than market chatter. The product prioritises relevant information at the decision point and avoids dressing speculation up as certainty.

02 / CLARITY

Clarity over complexity

We show the reason behind a verdict and the factors that contributed to it. A number without its context is not a useful answer.

03 / AGENCY

Your decision, always

IPO Advisor does not place orders, connect to a broker or tell you what you must do. It informs your research; you retain control over your capital.

04 / HONESTY

Uncertainty belongs in the product

When data is insufficient or reliability has not been demonstrated, the product withholds its probability. This is a commitment to usefulness over performance theatre.

ADVISORY ONLY

Research support, not financial advice.

IPO Advisor provides information and analytical context for research purposes. Investing in IPOs involves risk, and past outcomes do not guarantee future results.
