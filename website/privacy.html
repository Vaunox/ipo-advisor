Privacy Policy

IPO Advisor is a desktop application published by Quant Genesis. We have designed it to respect your privacy by collecting as little as possible.

What we collect: nothing.

IPO Advisor does not require an account, does not ask for personal information, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. The app runs on your device and displays publicly available information about Indian mainboard IPOs. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party data collection.

Data on your device.

Any settings or preferences you set (such as theme or display options) are stored locally on your own computer and never leave your device. We have no access to them.

Network activity.

To show current IPO information, the app fetches publicly available market data from the internet. This involves standard network requests to retrieve that information; it does not send any personal information about you.

Children.

IPO Advisor is intended for adults making their own research decisions and is not directed at children.

Changes.

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.
