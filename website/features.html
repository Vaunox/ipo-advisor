PRODUCT FEATURES

Everything you need
to read the signal.

A focused set of tools for following mainboard IPOs—designed to turn information into understandable context, without the distraction of a crowded trading interface.

Useful where the decision happens.

Each feature makes IPO research easier to follow and interrogate. Nothing inside the app is an order button, and no part replaces your judgement.

01 / LIVE

Live signals

See relevant, current verdicts in one decision board. Verdict, calibrated context and the grounded reason sit alongside each other.

02 / UPCOMING

IPO calendar

Keep upcoming and active issues in view with opening windows, anchor-day context, issue structure and valuation information—before a verdict is available.

03 / DETAIL

Explainable decision detail

Open any issue to see the signals and contributions behind the verdict, along with subscription context, watch items and changes over time.

04 / ALLOTMENT

Retail allotment context

Understand that a favourable outlook and receiving an allotment are different questions. The app makes that distinction visible rather than merging them into one number.

05 / HISTORY

Outcome and reliability history

Review the past record: the original view, the eventual outcome and how predicted probabilities compare to realised results over time.

DESIGNED WITH RESTRAINT

The app says “not enough signal” when that is the honest answer.

A missing or withheld number is a feature, not a failure. Probabilities are shown only when the reliability gate is satisfied.
